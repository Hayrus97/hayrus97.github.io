Crowdsourced Beer Recommendation System

Author: Suryah V | Master’s in Business Analytics @ UT Austin

Overview

This project builds the foundation of a crowdsourced recommendation system by scraping and analyzing beer reviews from BeerAdvocate. The goal is to recommend beers based on user-specified attributes such as “fruity,” “crisp,” or “robust,” going beyond simple popularity rankings.

Key Features

  • Scraped 8–10k reviews across ~250 beers using Selenium and BeautifulSoup
  • Cleaned and structured dataset into product, review, and rating fields
  • Implemented multiple recommendation approaches:
    • Bag-of-Words + Cosine Similarity + Sentiment
    • Pre-trained Word Embeddings (SpaCy)
    • Custom Embeddings (SpaCy/Gensim)
  • Compared attribute-based recommendations with simple popularity-based rankings

Results

Attribute-driven recommenders produced more relevant results than popularity rankings. Custom embeddings captured domain-specific vocabulary, while pre-trained embeddings provided semantic depth.
